# Misuzu Music 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

_最近更新 / Last Updated: 2025-11-15_

## 1. 概述 Overview
**中文：** Misuzu Music（以下简称“本应用”）是一款本地音乐播放器，旨在帮助您管理、播放并为曲库补充歌词与封面。我们尊重并保护您的隐私，只在提供核心功能所必需的范围内处理数据。
**English:** Misuzu Music ("the App") is a local music player that helps you manage playback and enrich tracks with lyrics and artwork. We respect your privacy and only process data that is strictly required to deliver the core experience.

## 2. 我们收集的数据 What We Collect
**中文：**
- 本地音乐资料（如歌曲文件、歌单、播放记录）仅保存在您的设备上，不会上传至我们的服务器。
- 当您启用自动获取歌词或封面时，本应用会把歌曲的标题、艺人或专辑等元数据发送到所选第三方内容提供方，以寻找匹配资源。
- 若您配置了自有 WebDAV 或其他网络存储，相关服务器地址与凭证仅以安全形式存储在本地，用于连接您指定的服务。
**English:**
- Local library information (track files, playlists, play history) stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
- If you opt in to automatic lyrics or artwork retrieval, the App sends track metadata (title, artist, album) to the chosen third-party content providers to locate matches.
- When you connect a self-hosted WebDAV or similar storage, the server address and credentials are stored securely on-device and are used only to access the service you selected.

## 3. 数据使用方式 How We Use Data
**中文：** 收集的数据仅用于：提供音乐播放、歌单管理、桌面歌词等功能；在您请求时检索歌词或封面；保持应用运行所需的缓存。我们不会将数据用于广告、画像或出售给第三方。
**English:** We use the collected information solely to deliver playback, playlist management, desktop lyrics, and the lyric/artwork lookup you request, plus essential caching. We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to anyone.

## 4. 数据存储与安全 Storage & Security
**中文：** 所有应用设置和媒体信息都保存在设备沙盒内或由您掌控的服务器上。系统级加密和平台安全机制（如 iOS Keychain、macOS Keychain 等）用于保护敏感凭证。我们定期审查权限与依赖，降低泄露风险。
**English:** App settings and media data remain within the device sandbox or on servers you control. Platform security controls (e.g., iOS/macOS Keychain) protect sensitive credentials, and we review permissions and dependencies to reduce exposure.

## 5. 第三方服务 Third-Party Services
**中文：** 为补全歌词、封面或其他元数据，本应用可能访问第三方内容提供方或开放 API。我们仅共享开展该请求所需的最小信息，并遵守对应服务条款。请参阅各服务的隐私政策以了解其处理方式。
**English:** To deliver lyrics, artwork, or other metadata, the App may reach out to third-party content providers or open APIs. Only the minimum necessary metadata is shared, and their own privacy policies apply to how they handle it.

## 6. 儿童隐私 Children's Privacy
**中文：** 本应用面向一般受众，不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计。若您认为我们在不知情情况下处理了儿童数据，请立即联系我们，我们会尽快删除相关信息。
**English:** The App targets a general audience and is not designed for children under 13. If you believe we inadvertently processed a child’s data, please contact us so we can remove it promptly.

## 7. 您的权利 Your Choices
**中文：** 您可以在应用设置中禁用网络检索功能、清除缓存或删除应用来移除全部本地数据。若您配置了自有服务器，也可随时更改或撤销凭证。
**English:** You may disable online lookups, clear caches, or uninstall the App to remove local data. For self-hosted services, you can modify or revoke stored credentials at any time.

## 8. 政策更新 Policy Updates
**中文：** 我们可能根据功能或法规变化调整本政策。重大变更会在应用内或项目主页醒目位置提醒，并更新“最近更新”日期。继续使用即表示您同意新的条款。
**English:** We may revise this Policy as features or regulations evolve. Material changes will be highlighted in-app or on the project site and the “Last Updated” date will change. Using the App after changes means you accept the revised terms.

## 9. 联系我们 Contact Us
**中文：** 如对隐私有任何问题、请求或投诉，请通过 GitHub Issues 提交（https://github.com/MCDFsteve/MisuzuMusic/issues），我们将在 30 天内回复。
**English:** For privacy questions, requests, or complaints, please open a GitHub Issue at https://github.com/MCDFsteve/MisuzuMusic/issues; we aim to respond within 30 days.
